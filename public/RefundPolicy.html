Refund Policy for Ubod

Last Updated: August 30, 2025

Ubod Service strives to provide a high-quality experience with Ubod, including both free and premium features. This Refund Policy explains how subscription payments and in-app purchases are handled and under what circumstances refunds may be issued.

1. Subscriptions

a. In-App Subscriptions (Apple App Store / Google Play Store)

  • Subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store are governed by the respective platform’s refund policies.
  • Ubod Service does not directly process refunds for in-app purchases made via these platforms.
  • Users requesting a refund for in-app purchases must contact Apple or Google support through their respective refund request process.

b. Website Subscriptions (via Paddle)

  • Subscriptions purchased on the Ubod website through Paddle are subject to our refund policy below.
  • You may request a refund within 14 days of the initial subscription payment for a full refund, provided that the service has not been extensively used.
  • Refunds may be partial if the service has been significantly used or accessed, at the discretion of Ubod Service.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • Any free trials or promotional offers are non-refundable.
  • Payments for expired or canceled subscriptions that have already provided access to premium features are generally non-refundable, except as required by law.
  • Any content or features consumed within the app (e.g., AI-generated recipes, nutritional tracking) are considered delivered and therefore typically non-refundable.

3. How to Request a Refund

  • For Paddle purchases via the Ubod website: Contact support at support@ubod.app with your account details, purchase date, and reason for the refund request.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed by Ubod Service and, if approved, processed through Paddle. Refunds typically take 5–10 business days to appear in your account.

4. Exceptions

Ubod Service reserves the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis, particularly in cases of technical issues, double charges, or other valid circumstances.
