Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

1. Personal Data

 When using Ubod, Ubod Service may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Mailing address (street, city, state/province, postal/ZIP code)
  • Payment or billing information (if applicable for subscriptions or premium services)
  • Other information you choose to share with us voluntarily (e.g., through forms, surveys, or support requests)

2. Usage Data

Ubod automatically collects certain information whenever you interact with the app. This data is necessary for functionality, analytics, and service improvement.

Usage Data may include:

  • Device Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages and features accessed, date and time of use, time spent in-app
  • Unique device identifiers and diagnostic data

When you access Ubod through a mobile device, additional data may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Mobile device type and unique ID
  • Mobile operating system and browser type
  • Mobile network information
  • Diagnostic and performance data related to app usage

3. Information from Third-Party Social Media Services

Ubod allows you to create an account or log in using third-party social media services, including:

  • Google
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn

If you choose to register or connect through one of these services, Ubod Service may collect personal data already linked to your account—such as your name, email address, profile picture, activity, or contact lists.

You may also choose to share additional information through these accounts. By doing so, you authorize Ubod Service to collect, use, and store this information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

4. Optional Data (with Consent)

Certain features of Ubod may request additional permissions or data. These are optional and require your consent before collection:

  • Location Data: Ubod may request access to your device’s location (approximate or precise) to provide location-based features such as nearby food database results, local nutritional insights, or personalized recommendations. You can enable or disable location access through your device settings.
  • Camera and Photos: Ubod may request access to your device’s camera or photo library to allow barcode scanning, receipt scanning, or food image uploads for AI analysis. Access is only used for the intended feature and can be managed in your device settings.
  • Health and Activity Data (if connected): If you choose to connect Ubod with third-party health or fitness apps (such as Apple Health or Google Fit), Ubod Service may collect health-related metrics (e.g., calories burned, steps, weight logs) to provide a more complete nutritional profile. This data is only collected with your explicit consent and can be disconnected at any time.

5. Information You Provide Directly

 You may also provide Ubod with additional personal data, such as dietary preferences, weight goals, or wellness objectives, to enable customized features and recommendations. Providing this information is optional, but certain features of Ubod may not function without it.

Ubod Service may use the personal data collected through Ubod for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain Ubod: Including account functionality, personalization, and monitoring usage to ensure the Service operates securely and effectively.
  • To manage your account: To handle your registration and maintain your user profile. The data you provide allows access to different features and settings within Ubod.
  • To fulfill contracts: To develop, perform, and manage subscription agreements, in-app purchases, or any other contracts entered into through Ubod.
  • To communicate with you: To contact you by email, phone, SMS, or push notifications regarding updates, account activity, or important information related to Ubod, including security alerts when necessary.
  • To provide offers and updates: To send you news, special offers, promotions, or general information about Ubod and related services—unless you have opted out.
  • To manage support requests: To receive, process, and respond to your inquiries or customer support requests.
  • For business operations: To evaluate or carry out a merger, acquisition, restructuring, sale, or other transfer of assets. In such cases, user data may be among the assets transferred, subject to this Privacy Policy.
  • For analytics and improvement: To analyze trends, evaluate the effectiveness of features or campaigns, improve Ubod’s performance, develop new offerings, and enhance the overall user experience.
  • For compliance and legal obligations: To meet regulatory, legal, or tax obligations, or to enforce Ubod Service’s terms and conditions.
  • With your consent: For any other specific purpose, but only if you have given explicit consent.

Your personal data may be shared in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: To support the operation of Ubod, including hosting, analytics, payment processing, communication, and customer support.
  • For business transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of assets, financing, or acquisition of Ubod Service or its assets by another company.
  • With Affiliates: Ubod Service may share data with its affiliates, subsidiaries, and parent company, provided they respect this Privacy Policy.
  • With business partners: To offer you specific promotions, bundled services, or other joint initiatives.

  • With your consent: In any other instance where you have expressly agreed to the sharing.

Retention and Security of Your Personal Data

1. Retention of Personal Data

Ubod Service will retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may retain personal data to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes or enforce our agreements and policies
  • Improve, maintain, and secure Ubod

Usage Data may be retained for a shorter period, typically for internal analysis, performance optimization, and security purposes. Certain Usage Data may be retained longer if legally required or necessary for ongoing service improvements.

2. Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be processed at Ubod Service’s operating offices and by third-party partners located in jurisdictions outside your home country. Laws regarding data protection may differ in these locations.

By using Ubod and submitting your information, you consent to such transfers. Ubod Service takes all reasonable steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including requiring appropriate safeguards for transfers to countries or organizations with differing data protection laws.

3. Deletion of Personal Data

You have the right to request deletion of the personal data Ubod Service holds about you.

  • Some data can be deleted directly from within Ubod by accessing your account settings.
  • You may contact Ubod Service to request access, correction, or deletion of any personal information you have provided.

Please note that certain data may need to be retained to comply with legal obligations or for legitimate business purposes, even after you request deletion.

4. Disclosure of Personal Data

  • Business Transactions: If Ubod Service is involved in a merger, acquisition, sale, or transfer of assets, your personal data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Law Enforcement: Ubod Service may disclose personal data when required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.
  • Other Legal Requirements: Ubod Service may disclose personal data in good faith to:
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect and defend Ubod Service’s rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential wrongdoing or security breaches
  • Protect users or the public from harm
  • Limit legal liability

5. Security of Personal Data

Ubod Service prioritizes the protection of your personal data. We implement commercially reasonable security measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

However, no electronic transmission or storage system is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
